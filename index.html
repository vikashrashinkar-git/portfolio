I'm Vikash Rashinkar — an AI-First Product Manager at IDS, where I build smarter, user-centered products powered by AI and automation. My journey started in Animation, VFX, and Lighting for feature films and commercials, where I learned the power of storytelling, precision, and user emotion.

Today I bring that same mindset into product building — combining AI, automation, and blockchain technologies to deliver scalable, human-centric solutions. My edge: I bridge creativity and systems thinking. From crafting cinematic visuals that tell stories, to designing products that solve real-world problems at scale. I believe great products are built at the intersection of empathy, technology, and execution.
