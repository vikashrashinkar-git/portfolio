Web3 Product Strategy Figma Roadmapping User Research 3D Design Animation Viscom Growth

I'm Vikash Rashinkar — an Associate Product Manager at IDS, a blockchain and Web3 firm, where I sit at the intersection of product strategy and visual design. Before this, I spent years as a 3D designer and animator, training my eye to see systems, flows, and friction that most PMs miss.

I hold a Master's in Design from MIT Institute of Design, Pune, and a background in Visual Communication. That combination means I can think in user journeys and render them. I write PRDs in the morning and can prototype in the afternoon.

My edge: I understand how things look and how they work — and I use both to make better product decisions.
